Welcome to StuDeo

If you are zealous or eager for knowledge you are in the right spot. Studeo or Studere the Latin word related to the noun studium, which means “zeal, eagerness, fondness.” Thus, ideally, as “student” is someone full of zeal and eagerness to know the true, good and beautiful, or a zeal for knowledge, virtue, understanding and wisdom..
