Course Description

In this comprehensive guide, you will learn everything you need to know to build your own custom PC. From defining your needs and choosing the right components to assembling and testing your build, you'll get hands-on experience and expert guidance every step of the way.

Course Modules

  1. Define your Needs: Determine your intended use for the PC and create a list of necessary components.
  2. Determine the Form Factor: Choose a form factor based on your needs and the size of your components.
  3. Choose Your Components: Select and purchase the components you need for your build, including a motherboard, processor, RAM, storage (SSD or HDD), power supply unit (PSU), and case.
  4. Build Your PC: Assemble your PC by following step-by-step instructions for installing the motherboard, processor, RAM, storage, PSU, and case components.
  5. Test and Enjoy: Power on your PC and test that all components are working correctly. Install your operating system and necessary drivers.
